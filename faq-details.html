How to perform routine maintenance on cloud infrastructure?

Authored by Cloud Security Team
12 Apr, 2020

To enable MFA, you must have a secure authentication app installed on your smartphone. Recommended apps include Google Authenticator or Authy, available for both iOS and Android.

  • Visit 'Account Settings' in your Cloud Maintenance Dashboard.
  • Under 'Multi-Factor Authentication,' click 'Activate MFA.'
  • In your authentication app, add a new login and scan the provided QR code. Follow the prompts to complete the setup.
  • Enter the generated code from the app into the designated box and click 'Activate MFA.'

Note: If you lose access to your MFA app, contact our support for a security assessment and to unlock your account.
