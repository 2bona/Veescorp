Our Journey

The Industry's Pioneer in Cloud Maintenance Solutions

We founded Bulidate for businesses that share our passion for maintaining cloud infrastructure. For many of us, excellent cloud maintenance isn’t just a service — it’s a strategic asset, a competitive advantage, and a foundation of trust.

But in 2008, there were no cloud maintenance platforms available that embodied our customer-centric values. There were solutions that treated each task as a transaction and made it overly complex to establish long-term relationships. We felt a strong desire to create something different, and we did.
